Grilled Cheese

How to make it

Timeless yet always delicious, a grilled cheese sandwich will always hit the spot!

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of your choice of cheese
  • butter

  1. Spread a thin layer of butter onto 1 side of each slice of bread.
  2. Lay 2 slices of bread on a work surface, buttered side down. Place a slice of cheese on the center of each slice of bread.
  3. Put the bread together with the cheese inside, cook each buttered side of the bread in a pan on medium high hear until golden brown.
